Welcome to Bias Buster

This is a simple web application to help you identify and mitigate bias in your dataset.

To get started, please upload your dataset.

Instructions

1. Upload your dataset in CSV or Excel format.

2. The application will analyze the dataset for potential biases.

3. Review the results and take necessary actions to mitigate bias.

About

Bias Buster is designed to help data scientists and researchers identify biases in their datasets, ensuring fair and equitable outcomes in machine learning models.
